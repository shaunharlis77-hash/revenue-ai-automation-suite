# Human Review Policy

## Principle

AI should assist sales operations work, not silently replace human judgment.

## Review Required

Human review should be required before:

- Sending customer-facing messages.
- Updating CRM records.
- Changing lead scores used for prioritization.
- Sharing meeting summaries outside the internal team.
- Producing proposals or commercial terms.
- Using AI output in decisions that affect customers or revenue reporting.

## Reviewer Responsibilities

Reviewers should check accuracy, tone, completeness, source context, and business judgment. They should be able to edit, reject, or approve any AI-assisted output.

## Future Product Requirements

The dashboard should eventually show:

- What was generated.
- What sources were used.
- Who reviewed it.
- What changed during review.
- When it was approved or rejected.

## Current Status

This foundation includes placeholder review concepts only. No approval system is implemented yet.
